Privacy Policy & Data Handling (GDPR & Spotify Compliance)

  • Local Data: By default, all data retrieved from the Spotify API is stored locally in your browser's cache to authenticate your session and render metrics.
  • Cloud Backups (Optional): If you explicitly toggle "Cloud Backup" in your profile settings, your listening history and stats snapshots are synchronized to our secure cloud database. This enables multi-device caching and automated daily sync processes.
  • Right to Erasure (GDPR Article 17): You can completely erase all your data at any time:
    • Cloud Data: Click the profile icon, select Clear Data, and choose Delete Cloud Backup to permanently wipe all synced history, snapshots, and caches from our database.
    • Local Cache & Logout: Select Clear Local Cache & Logout to wipe all browser-level cache and terminate the session.
    • Revoke App Access: Click the "Revoke App Access" link in the profile settings dropdown to manage permissions directly on the Spotify Apps dashboard.
